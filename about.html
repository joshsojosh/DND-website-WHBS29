About Character Creator

Welcome to Character Creator, a web application designed to help you create and manage characters for your tabletop role-playing games, stories, or creative projects.

Our character creator allows you to:

  • Create characters with multiple classes (up to a total of level 20)
  • Customize attributes, appearance, and background
  • Save characters to your browser's local storage
  • Export characters as JSON files for sharing or backup
  • View and manage all your created characters in one place

How to Use

Getting started with Character Creator is easy:

  1. Go to the Create Character page
  2. Fill in your character's details, including name, race, and classes
  3. Customize attributes, appearance, and background
  4. Click "Save Character" to store your creation
  5. View all your characters in the Character Gallery

Multi-Classing

Our character creator supports multi-classing with a maximum total level of 20 across all classes. This means you can create diverse character builds like:

  • Fighter (Level 10) / Wizard (Level 10)
  • Rogue (Level 3) / Bard (Level 5) / Warlock (Level 12)
  • Barbarian (Level 5) / Druid (Level 5) / Monk (Level 5) / Ranger (Level 5)

The system will automatically ensure you don't exceed the level 20 cap.

Privacy

Character Creator uses your browser's local storage to save your characters. This means:

  • Your data stays on your device and is not sent to any server
  • Your characters will persist between sessions on the same browser
  • Clearing your browser data will remove your saved characters
  • We recommend exporting important characters as JSON files for backup
